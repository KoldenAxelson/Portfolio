This is a placeholder marker. To enable OpenGraph and Twitter Card
previews:

  1. Make a 1200×630 PNG (≤100 KB recommended).
  2. Save it as public/og-default.png.
  3. In src/config.ts, set DEFAULT_OG_IMAGE = '/og-default.png'.
  4. Delete this .txt file.

Until then, the SEO component skips emitting og:image / twitter:image
meta tags entirely — better than shipping broken-image links to social
platforms.

Pages can also override per-page by passing the `image` prop to
BaseLayout.

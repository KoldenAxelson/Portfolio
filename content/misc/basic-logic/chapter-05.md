## Key ideas

The heart of the summary. Walk through the chapter's main moves in plain
language. A **statement** is a sentence that is either true or false — never
both, never neither. "Paso Robles is in California" is a statement. "Close the
door" is not, and neither is "What time is it?"

Introduce terms in **bold** the first time they appear, and lean on short,
concrete examples rather than abstract definitions.
